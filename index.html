Key Characters

The souls that bring ASTITVA to life

Arjun

The Protagonist

An orphan with no past. Framed, imprisoned, memory erased. A man who rebuilds himself through love and truth. His journey is from "Who am I?" to reclaiming his existence.

Danya

The Soul

Innocent, loving, and strong. Her suffering becomes the emotional soul of the story. Even in a coma, her presence anchors Arjun's reality. Love is her resistance.

M

Mohit

The Betrayer

Arjun's only friend. Consumed by jealousy over Danya's love for Arjun. Betrayal disguised as loyalty. His actions set the tragic events in motion.

R

Rakesh

The Power

Son of political power. Represents arrogance, entitlement, and unchecked authority. Believes the world exists only to serve him and his friends.

A

Arun

The Corruption

Son of police power. Law turned shield for crime. Uses his family's influence to manipulate evidence and ensure the false narrative stands.

D

Danush

The Privilege

Son of political privilege. Violence without consequence‚Äîuntil truth returns. Part of the powerful trio that believes they are above the law.
